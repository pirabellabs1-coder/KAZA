1. PARTIES

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made this 15th day of September, 2023, by and between Skyline Property Management LLC ("Landlord") and Alex J. Rivera ("Tenant").

2. PREMISES

Landlord hereby leases to Tenant and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord the following real property: 452 Oak Avenue, Unit 3B, San Francisco, CA 94102.

3. TERM

The initial term of this lease shall be for 12 months, commencing on October 1, 2023, and ending on September 30, 2024. Upon expiration of the term, this Agreement shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis unless either party provides 30 days' written notice of termination.

4. RENT PAYMENTS

Tenant shall pay to Landlord a monthly rent of $2,400.00, payable in advance on the first day of every month. Payments shall be made via the KAZA Proptech portal or as otherwise directed by Landlord. A late fee of $100.00 shall be charged for any payment received after the 5th day of the month.

5. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of $4,800.00 as security for any damage caused to the Premises during the term hereof or any other default by Tenant. This deposit will be returned to Tenant, less any allowable deductions, within 21 days after Tenant vacates the premises.
